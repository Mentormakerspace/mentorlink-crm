# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Internal MentorLink sales reps, admins, and owners tracking deals through a pipeline. Used at a desk during active sales work — entering deals, checking payment status, moving prospects through stages. The primary task on any screen is "what do I need to do next on this deal?"

## Product Purpose

MentorLink CRM is an internal deal pipeline tool for the MentorLink team. It tracks companies from first contact through closed deals, manages payment milestones, and gives reps and managers visibility into pipeline health. Success means reps spend time selling, not hunting for deal status.

## Brand Personality

Confident, clean, focused. Three words: sharp, trustworthy, efficient.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS purple-gradient dashboards with hero metrics and big numbers
- Rainbow button palettes (indigo create, green add, red logout — all different colors)
- Cluttered admin UIs that feel like internal tools built in 2015
- Muted gray placeholder states with no character

## Design Principles

1. **One job per screen** — each view should have one obvious primary action; secondary actions recede
2. **Status at a glance** — deal health, payment status, and stage should be readable without clicking
3. **Brand discipline** — one primary color, used intentionally; not a different color per button
4. **Trust through precision** — numbers, dates, and statuses must be accurate and clearly labeled
5. **Earn the space** — no placeholder sections; if a component has no data, say so clearly

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Keyboard navigable. Color is never the only indicator of status.
